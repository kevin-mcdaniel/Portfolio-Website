Other Stuff I Do

I'm an avid backpacker, and in 2019 I hiked over 2000 miles on the Pacific Crest Trail and in 2021 I hiked all 486 miles of the Colorado Trail. I also do weekend excursions and day hikes all over Oregon on a regular basis. I spend so much time outdoors that I have taken to documenting my adventures on Youtube.

Working on my channel has fueled my interest in both video editing and filmmaking. While I don't spend a lot of time on it, I hope to someday grow my channel into something that might become more of a full time gig. For now I content myself with it just being a hobby.

A lot of my working career has revolved around motorsports and the automotive industry so as you can imagine, I have lots of intrests in that regard. Overlanding seems a logical offshoot of my interest in camping and backpacking so I am in the process of modifying my 2006 Subaru Outback into a more capable trail vehicle. I'm hoping at some point in the near future to participate in the Gambler 500.

Lastly, I'm a big gamer of all kinds. I enjoy tabletop, card, roleplaying and video games. I enjoy reading, playing guitar and am even a pretty good cook. I actually have so many hobbies that I sometimes have a hard time focusing.
